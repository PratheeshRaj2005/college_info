Scholarship Opportunities

Excellence in Education Scholarship

Amount: $10,000

Deadline: December 1, 2024

Eligibility Criteria:

  • Minimum GPA: 3.5
  • Must be enrolled in a full-time undergraduate program
  • Open to students in any field of study

How to Apply:

Submit your application online with your academic transcripts and a personal essay outlining your goals and achievements.

Women in STEM Scholarship

Amount: $5,000

Deadline: November 15, 2024

Eligibility Criteria:

  • Female students pursuing a degree in a STEM field
  • Minimum GPA: 3.2
  • Demonstrated leadership in school or the community

How to Apply:

Submit your application online with a project portfolio and a letter of recommendation from a professor or employer.

Future Innovators Grant

Amount: $15,000

Deadline: January 10, 2025

Eligibility Criteria:

  • Open to all undergraduate students
  • Must have an innovative project or research in progress
  • Minimum GPA: 3.0

How to Apply:

Submit a detailed project proposal and a letter from your faculty mentor supporting your application.
