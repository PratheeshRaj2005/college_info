Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for admission, the student must have completed 12th grade (or equivalent) with at least 60% marks in the following subjects:

  • Mathematics
  • Physics
  • Chemistry

In addition, the student must have a valid score in any national or state-level engineering entrance exam.

Admission Process

The admission process consists of the following steps:

  • Submit the online application form through our website.
  • Upload the required documents such as 12th grade marksheet, entrance exam scorecard, etc.
  • Appear for the personal interview, if shortlisted.
  • Final selection will be based on academic performance, entrance exam score, and the interview.

Important Dates

  • Application Start Date: May 1, 2024
  • Application Deadline: June 30, 2024
  • Interview Dates: July 15 - 20, 2024
  • Final Selection Announcement: August 5, 2024
